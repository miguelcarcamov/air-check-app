"×" shows PM2.5 as a multiple of WHO's 2021 24-hour guideline (15 µg/m³). Outdoor PM2.5 comes from the nearest real SINCA government monitoring station when one is within 30 km (marked ●); otherwise it falls back to a modeled estimate from Open-Meteo/CAMS (marked ○). Modeled estimates are known to carry meaningful, region-dependent bias in published validation studies (roughly 5–20%+ in regions that have been studied), but no dedicated validation exists for Chile specifically — treat a ○ reading as a rough guide, not a precise number. Ozone always comes from the model. Indoor reading is whatever you last logged from your own sensor.
